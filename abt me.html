about me :)

hi, I'm kelly! i'm 19 and from Paris.
i love creating images, especially through film photography.
capturing moments of my life and the emotions they hold is something that deeply inspires me.
music also has a huge place in my life, shaping my experiences and often influencing my creative process.
my work often explores the theme of identity, especially from the perspective of a racialized child growing up in a country like france.
i feel a strong connection to my asian roots and am committed to celebrating and highlighting my culture through my art.
